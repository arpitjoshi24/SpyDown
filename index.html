Our games

Golu

Golu challenges players to navigate levels using a grappling hook to swing between platforms, all while avoiding hazards and making strategic use of breaking platforms. Simple controls and progressively difficult levels promise an engaging and exciting gameplay experience.

Letter Quest

Letter Quest is a mobile game designed to transform playtime into learning time for young children (ages 4-5) by using a drag-and-drop mechanic to teach letter recognition, basic spelling through word completion, and vocabulary building through image association.

Escape

An immersive journey of mystery "Escape," a door puzzle game that challenges your mind and puzzle-solving skills. Locked in a house, your only way out is to uncover hidden passwords and locate keys to unlock the doors

Smash It

In this destructive game, Player have to smash/push the opponent's car using joystick control. This is a localmultiplayer game and also a single player game(against AI).
